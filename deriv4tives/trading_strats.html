Definition

Holding of different derivatives in one portfolio.


Put-Call parity

  • Long: 1 call with strike K (95).
  • Short: 1 put with strike K (95).
  • Same payoff as futures contract with the same strike.
Straddle

  • Long: 1 call with strike K (100).
  • Long: 1 put with strike K (100).
  • Long volatility strategy, i.e. portfolio benefits from large market movements in both directions.
Strangle
  • Long: 1 call with strike K1 (110).
  • Long: 1 put with strike K2 (95) such that K1 > K2.
  • Long volatility strategy.
Strip
  • Long: 1 call with strike K (95).
  • Long: 2 puts with strike K (95).
  • Long volatility strategy.

Welcome to Deriv4tives

Financial markets are full of assets that participants buy and sell everyday.

However, things are not limited to those assets.

Other instruments called derivatives have been created based on the value of the assets mentioned above.

As their name suggests, derivatives derive their value from their underlying asset.

This site covers the main derivatives, some pricing methodologies and examples of trading strategies are provided.

Enough of talking: let's dive into the world of derivatives.

Delta one

Financial instruments whose value is linearly impacted * by any price movement of the underlying *.


Glossary
  • Linear impact

    • Term describing a proportional variation in one quantity.


  • Underlying

    • Asset used to compute a derivative value.

Futures
  • Agreement to buy or sell an underlying at a predefined price at some point in the future.

  • Standarised, i.e. traded on exchanges.

  • Contract size indicates the amount of the underlying that has to be delivered.

  • Strike at 95.
Forwards
  • Agreement to buy or sell an underlying at a predefined price at some point in the future.

  • Bespoke contract agreed on by both sides.

  • Traded over-the-counter (OTC).

  • Strike at 95.
